Tigers have soft toe pads which help them walk silently through their habitat. A tiger will typically travel 6-12 miles during a night's hunting.

BEAR

The bear belongs to the family Ursidae. Bears are carnivores but they also feed on leaves and fruits. Bears include sloth bears, giant pandas, polar bears, Asian black bears, and American black bears. They can climb with ease and can swim. They undergo hibernation or deep sleep during winter.
